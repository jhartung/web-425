Danny Elfman



Danny Elfman (1953-Present) is the former lead singer of the 1980s New Wave band Oingo Boingo and a prominent film and television composer. As a composer, he wrote the themes to the Simpsons, Tim Burton's Batman movies, Sam Raimi's Spider-man movies, the Nightmare Before Christmas (where he also served as the singing voice of protagonist Jack Skellington), and much more. He is recently released a new solo album in 2021 titled Big Mess, and performed at the 2022 Coachella Music Festival.
